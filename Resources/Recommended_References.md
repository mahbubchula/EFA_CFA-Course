## Reporting Standards for Publications

### Transportation Journals
- *Transportation Research Part A/B/C/D* — APA 7th edition
- *Accident Analysis & Prevention* — APA 7th edition
- *Journal of Advanced Transportation* — APA or Vancouver
- *Travel Behaviour and Society* — APA or Elsevier guidelines

### Behavioral Research Journals
- *Transportation Research Part F (Traffic Psychology)* — APA 7th
- *Journal of Transport & Health* — Vancouver style

### Reporting Checklists
- **CFA/SEM**: Report χ²/df, CFI, TLI, RMSEA (+CI), SRMR; all factor loadings; CR, AVE; HTMT
- **Regression**: Report B (or OR), SE, t/z, p, R² (or Nagelkerke R²), F or Wald
- **EFA**: Report KMO, Bartlett, all factor loadings (≥ 0.40), eigenvalues, % variance
